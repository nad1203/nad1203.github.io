    About Me



    Hi! Nice to meet you, I'm Nadya Audrey.

    Currently working as a UI/UX Designer at Applied Medical, in sunny California.

    I'm a UX Designer with a Computer Science and Human-Computer Interaction background. To me, designing isn't just about making things pretty. Designing includes researching and brainstorming to solve the problems we're facing, which doesn't always have something to do with the looks. My design decisions are usually data-driven because I believe that data could expose problems and allow us to evaluate the effectiveness of solutions. I love solving problems, collaborate, and exchange ideas with my team because I believe that is an invaluable opportunity to learn something new.

    A little bit about my educational background, I graduated from the University of California, San Diego with a degree in Human-Computer Interaction. I've always had an interest in design ever since I was young, but I decided to study computer science in community college. I learned about HCI major not long after, and I couldn't be happier when I found out that I could make use of my coding skills and also learn about design. And here I am living my dream.

    In my free time, you can find me baking, watercoloring, or exploring online classes to pick up new skills (fancy CSS tricks & illustration). But most often, watching movies or TV shows.
