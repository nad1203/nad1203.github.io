My name is Nadya Audrey, a UX Designer with a Computer Science and Human-Computer Interaction background. Currently, I am working as a UI/UX Designer at Applied Medical. I lead a couple of internal-facing projects and also maintain the team's design system. To me, designing is more than just making things pretty. Designing is about finding a problem and coming up with an elegant creative solution to solve it. I am also a strong believer that designers should have at least basic coding knowledge at hand. This skill will come in handy to design feasible solutions and to have effective communication with the developer team.

A little about my educational background, I graduated from the University of California, San Diego with a degree in Human-Computer Interaction. I've always had an interest in design ever since I was young but decided to study computer science during my first two years in college. I learned about the Human-Computer Interaction major not long after and I was so happy to find out that I could make use of my coding skills and learn about design. Long story short, here I am living my dream.

In my free time, you can find me baking, watercoloring, or most often, watching movies or TV shows.
